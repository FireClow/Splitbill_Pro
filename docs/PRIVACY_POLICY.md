# Privacy Policy - SplitBill

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

SplitBill ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process your information in relation to our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Bill and Split Information:** When you create bills, add items, and manage splits, we store titles, amounts, currencies, participant information, and payment details
- **Device Identifier:** We collect a unique device identifier to maintain your session and synchronize your data across devices
- **Account Information:** Email, names, contact information (optional)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Analytics:** Information about how you interact with the app, features you use, and error logs
- **IP Address:** Your IP address is collected for security and analytics purposes

### 2.3 Google AdMob Information

**Advertising Service:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our application.

**Ad Personalization & Data Collection:**
- **Google Advertising ID:** Google AdMob collects your Google Advertising ID to serve personalized ads
- **Advertising Data:** 
  - Device specifications (type, OS version)
  - Approximate location (city-level based on IP address)
  - App usage patterns and app interactions
  - Device activity (interactions with ads, install data)
  - Non-personalized demographic information

**How Google Uses Your Data:**
- Google uses the collected data to create interest-based audience segments
- Serves targeted advertisements based on your interests and behavior
- Measures ad performance and campaign effectiveness
- Prevents ad fraud and validates ad conversions
- Improves ad relevance and user experience

For more information about Google's privacy practices with AdMob, visit [Google AdMob Privacy & Security](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide Services:** Create and manage bill splits, calculate expenses, store transaction history
- **Display Advertisements:** Serve AdMob ads (you may see personalized or non-personalized ads)
- **Improve Services:** Analyze app performance, fix bugs, enhance user experience
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, ensure account security
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- **Communication:** Send important updates about the app (when necessary)

## 5. User Control & Ad Preferences

### 5.1 Ad Preferences

- **Personalized Ads Opt-Out:** You can opt out of personalized ads:
  - **Android:** Go to Settings > Google > Manage your Google Account > Data & Privacy > Ad settings
  - **iOS:** Go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads toggle OFF

- **AdMob Settings:** You can control ad frequency in app settings (Premium users see no ads)

- **Premium Users:** Users with a premium subscription will not see any advertisements

### 5.2 Data Access & Deletion

- **Access Your Data:** You can request a copy of your bill data
- **Delete Your Data:** You can request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Contact us** at [your-contact-email] to exercise these rights

## 6. Data Retention

- **Bill Information:** Retained for the duration of your account or as long as necessary for bill reconciliation
- **Device ID & Session Data:** Retained until account deletion
- **Advertising Data:** Google AdMob retains advertising data per their retention policies (typically 24+ months)

## 7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure storage of sensitive information
- Regular security audits
- User authentication via device ID

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please report any security concerns.

## 8. Children's Privacy

SplitBill is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

For users between 13-18 years old:
- Parental consent is required for personalized advertising in some jurisdictions
- We can provide a non-personalized ad version upon request

## 13. AdMob-Specific Information

### Ad Types Served

1. **Banner Ads:** Displayed at the bottom of screens (300x50 or adaptive size)
2. **Interstitial Ads:** Full-screen ads shown during app transitions
3. **Rewarded Ads:** Optional ads for unlocking premium features

### Ad Frequency

- Banner ads are shown on all non-premium user screens
- Interstitial ads are shown every 3 successful bill creations (non-premium users only)
- Premium users see zero advertisements

### Disabling Personalized Ads

If you disable personalized ads in your device settings, we will serve you non-personalized ads. This does not reduce ad frequency but treats ads as generic rather than tailored to your interests.

While AdMob is our primary network, we may integrate additional ad networks in the future. We will update this policy accordingly.
